about.

I am a designer and a full stack web developer in training with a passion for creating immersive digital experiences. My expertise lies in user-centered design, front-end and back-end technologies, allowing me to bring ideas to life through visually stunning and highly functional websites and applications.

Apart from web development, I have a deep interest in 3D art, where I enjoy crafting virtual objects. I find joy in the process of 3D modeling, and strive to create captivating visual experiences.

Additionally, I am fascinated by the Japanese language, anime, and music. I love exploring the rich cultural heritage of Japan and appreciate the artistry and storytelling found in anime. Music also holds a special place in my heart, and I enjoy listening to various genres while working or relaxing.
